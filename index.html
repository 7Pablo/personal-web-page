Showcase

Ternium App

Web application developed for Ternium Company using the React library with a focus on conducting an anomaly scan and rating algorithm on their historical data files. Kito & Bodoke

An online multiplayer puzzle platformer video game made in Unity. The game is aimed at kids and teenagers, where players can choose one of two characters with their own unique mechanics to traverse and complete each of the 20 levels as a team. The game includes account management, login, a menu, a map, decision making, and boss fights.
